About Us

We are Glasgow's first hipster laundrette, a hipster laundrette is somewhere you can go to relax and hang out while we do the hard work. We are a laundrette and a cafe merged into one! We have a high quality service, both with the washing and with our food. We specialise in cookies which are freshly made in shop each day, we also do personalised cookies for gifts or to be enjoyed in the cafe.

We have a range of different options for you to get your washing done with us, from washing, drying and dry cleaning there is nothing we cant do. Please feel free to swing by anytime to use our facilities, and have a coffee while you wait.

The Owners

We inherited this laundrette to bring a new and fresh way to think about doing laundry. We wanted to create a fun and friendly atmosphere for people to come and socialise while doing their laundry. With our renovations finally completed we can do just that!

Our new hipster laundrette is a place for everyone to come and enjoy a cup of coffee or a bite to eat while doing laundry.

Open Mic Nights

We have an Open Mic night every Thursday at 7pm. Please feel free to contact us to play at the venue! Anyone can Sign Up!

We have a stage situated in the cafe where we have people come and preform every Thursday. We are open until 10pm Thurs-Sunday with live preformances from local artists. There are open spots available, if you would like to equire about any open slots please get in contact with us.

The Sandemans

The Sandemans are a regular band who preform live at the laundrette. They are a group of students from the University of Glasgow that have now been playing together for 2 years.

Oz Mooney

Oz is a first year apprentice working towards their degree in nuclear engineering. In their spare time they love to play all sorts of instruments but specifically the guitar.

Cerys Kharoufeh

Cerys has been singing since she was 7 and has been singing through out many different places in Glasgow. Once a month she drops by ATCT to sing one of her own original songs.

Lara Havis

Lara is a 4th year student studying law with business, she is due to graduate this year. She loves to play guitar and cant wait to preform here with us at ATCT.

Location

You can find us located at 17 Derby Street. We are in a central location close to the university just off of Argyle Street, this makes it easy for students to come down without having to travel too far from their accomodation to get their laundry done. We are only 0.5 miles from the University of Glasgow, and 1.6 away from the University of Strathclyde. Being in the centre we are a mile away from Glasgow Central, making it easy to drop by before and after work.
